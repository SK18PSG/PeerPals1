PeerPals

An academic community, fostering and building a platform for all students alike!

Welcome to PeerPals, a haven for students one and all. PeerPals is a platform for you to engage in conversation with your school friends, peers, and even teachers! Catch up on missed classes, take your learning forward, and explore the academic world with Peer Pals! This platform is specially designed for new students, who may find their academic soulmate, and do not shy away from creating new connections, for who knows? You can make your peer a pal for life!
